About MyNotePad

MyNotPad is an open, editable notebook catalog, building towards a web page for easy access to notebad Just like the normal installed notepad, you can create new information or corrections to the your notepad. You can save your notes in cloud for as long as you wish, create novel or lists members you have created. If you love taking jots, why not have MyNotePad account?
